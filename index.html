    Tsaatan
    The Tsaatan live as nomads - moving camp several times a year,
    usually when the seasons change. They do this of necessity.
    If they stayed in their winter camp during the hot summer,
    their reindeer would become vulnerable to insects and parasites.

    North and lies in the historically significant Orkhon Valley,

    eruption and earthquakes some 20000 years ago cascades down from a height 27 meters.
